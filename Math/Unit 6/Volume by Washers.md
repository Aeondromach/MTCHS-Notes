# Volume by washers for Rotation about the x-axis
$$V=\int^b_a\pi([R(x)]^2-[r(x)]^2)dx$$
$R(x)=$ Outer radius
$r(x)=$ Inner radius
#### Example
Find the volume of the solid generated by revolving the region bounded by $y=x^2+1$ and $y=-x+3$ about the x-axis
