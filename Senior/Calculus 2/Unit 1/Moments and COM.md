# Center of Mass (COM)
The Point where an object will be balanced.

## Thin Plates in the x-y Plane and Center of Mass
Moment about the $x$-axis:$$Mx=\int y~dm$$
$$dm=\delta\text{ (density)}~ dA\text{ (area of a strip)}=\rho~dA$$
$$dA=l*w=f(x)~dx$$
