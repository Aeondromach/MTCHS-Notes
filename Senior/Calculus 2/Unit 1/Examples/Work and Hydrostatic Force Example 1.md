Find the work required to stretch a spring from it's natural length of $1ft$ to a length of $0.75ft$ if the force constant is $16\frac{lb}{ft}$.
$$F(x)=16*x$$
$$W=\int^.25_0 16x~dx$$
$$W=8x^2|^.25_0$$
$$W=\frac{8}{16}=.5\frac{ft}{lb}$$
